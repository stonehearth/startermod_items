Trapper Level Ups

The Trapper will now gain experience and level up as he traps critters. Levelling up offers new job-related abilities. In the citizen management pane, click the Trapper's banner icon to see their exp, level, and job abilities.

This is just the start of the job levelling system. Exepect a more in subesquent releases.
